Proven Research Management, Ethics Application Approval and Compliance Training Solutions

The efficient management of research, fast and secure clearance of ethics applications, and compliant clinical/research staff are essentials in today’s health research sector.

This is why Infonetica’s practical, proven and professional software solutions are relied on by thousands of research managers and ethics committees/institutional review boards in industry, research institutions and hospitals in the UK and around the world.

Our cost-effective software improves workflow, reduces administration time and cost, and brings clarity to the sometimes complex work of clinical research.

Infonetica is the leading global provider of such solutions and is meeting the needs of every type of organisation: from research management in a single hospital site, through online training provision for the pharmaceutical industry, to running national/regional government ethics approval systems.

Tap into our knowledge and experience today. Why not call for an online demonstration, arrange a site visit or engage in a no obligation discussion on how Infonetica can help in your organisation?
